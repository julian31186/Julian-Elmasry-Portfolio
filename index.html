Automatic COVID Form Filler

Currently at the job I work at, every day we come into work we must fill out a mandatory COVID screening form that asks the same questions everyday. When I know for certain that I can answer NO to the following questions and go into work as safe as possible, I created this script that simply fills out the form with the correct information when ran which saves me a few extra minutes in the morning.
